## van Genuchten 1980

$$
\frac{d \theta}{d \psi}=\frac{\alpha m n\left(\theta_{\text {sat }}-\theta_{\text {res }}\right)(\alpha|\psi|)^{n-1}}{\left[I+(\alpha|\psi|)^n\right]^{m+1}} \quad 
$$

$$
\begin{aligned}
\frac{d K}{d \theta} &=\frac{K_{\text {sat }}}{\left(\theta_{\text {sat }}-\theta_{\text {res }}\right)}\left[\frac{f^2}{2 S_e^{1 / 2}}+\frac{2 S_e^{1 / m-1 / 2} f}{\left(1-S_e^{1 / m}\right)^{1-m}}\right], \\
f &= 1-\left(1-S_e^{1 / m}\right)^m
\end{aligned}
$$


## Campbell 1974

$$
\frac{d \theta}{d \psi}=\frac{-\theta_{\text {sat }}}{b \psi_{\text {sat }}}\left(\frac{\psi}{\psi_{\text {sat }}}\right)^{-1 / b-1} \quad 
$$

$$
\frac{d K}{d \theta}=\frac{K_{\text {sat }}(2 b+3)}{\theta_{\text {sat }}}\left(\frac{\theta}{\theta_{\text {sat }}}\right)^{2 b+2}
$$
